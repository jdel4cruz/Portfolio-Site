About

I like learning new things. Having felt restrained in my former career as an engineer, I decided that it wasn't for me and that I wanted a career where I opportunites to learn are endless.

During school, the few coding assignments that were assigned to me were interesting, so so I figured that was a good place to start. Combining that with my desire to create things, it seemed like front-end web development might fit the bill. When looking into online courses, I found an online bootcamp on Udemy for 80% off and figured it be as good a place to start as any. And the rest is history.
