John De La Cruz

Web Developer

Former engineer driven by the pursuit of knowledge. Seeking new opportunities in the vast world of web development.

My name is John De La Cruz and I like learning new things. Having felt restrained in my former career as an engineer, I decided that it was not the path for me and decided to search for a career where I have the freedom both to create and expand my skillset as an individual.

During my time as a student, tasks that involved coding managed to maintain my interest so I figured that was a good place to start. Combining that with my desire to create things, it seemed like front-end web development might fit the bill. When looking into online courses, I found an online bootcamp on Udemy for 80% off and figured it be as good a place to start as any. And the rest is history.
